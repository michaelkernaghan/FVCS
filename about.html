About the Fraser Valley Croquet Society

A registered non-profit society dedicated to promoting croquet throughout the Fraser Valley region of British Columbia.

History & Founding

The Fraser Valley Croquet Society was established in 2023 and incorporated as a British Columbia registered society under registration number S0075824. The society was founded in Mission, BC, recognizing the need for a regional organization to support and coordinate croquet activities throughout the Fraser Valley.

As a registered non-profit society operating under the BC Societies Act, FVCS provides a formal organizational structure for croquet development, ensuring accountability, transparency, and sustainability for the sport in our region.

The society maintains its registered office in Mission, honoring its founding community while serving the broader Fraser Valley through member clubs and programs in multiple municipalities.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To promote, develop, and organize the sport of croquet throughout the Fraser Valley region, providing opportunities for recreation, competition, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To establish the Fraser Valley as a thriving croquet region with multiple active clubs, accessible community programs, and strong participation in national croquet activities.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Maintaining high standards in all programs
  • Accessibility: Making croquet available to all community members
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency and accountability
  • Community: Fostering social connections through sport

Affiliations & Partnerships

Croquet Canada

The Fraser Valley Croquet Society is a member of Croquet Canada, the national organization for croquet in Canada. This membership provides:

  • Access to national standards and best practices
  • Connection to the broader Canadian croquet community
  • Participation in national events and competitions
  • Resources for program development and coaching
Visit Croquet Canada

Municipal Partnerships

FVCS works collaboratively with municipal recreation departments throughout the Fraser Valley to deliver community croquet programs, including:

  • Parks and recreation facility access
  • Community education courses
  • Public engagement initiatives
  • Sport development partnerships

Accountability & Transparency

As a registered BC society, Fraser Valley Croquet Society is committed to operating with full transparency and accountability. We maintain compliance with all provincial requirements, including:

  • Annual filing requirements with BC Registry Services
  • Maintaining current and accurate society records
  • Financial accountability to members
  • Adherence to society bylaws and constitution
  • Regular reporting to members and stakeholders
