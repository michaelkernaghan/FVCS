Regional Croquet Organization

The Fraser Valley Croquet Society is a registered non-profit society dedicated to promoting and organizing croquet throughout the Fraser Valley region of British Columbia.

BC Society Registration: S0075824

Our Mission

To promote, develop, and organize the sport of croquet throughout the Fraser Valley region, providing opportunities for recreation, competition, and community engagement.

Regional Scope

As a regional society, FVCS serves the entire Fraser Valley, supporting multiple member clubs and programs across communities including Mission, Abbotsford, and surrounding areas.

Croquet Canada Member

The Fraser Valley Croquet Society is a member of Croquet Canada, the national organization for croquet in Canada.
