Visit Our Programs

The Fraser Valley Croquet Society welcomes visitors to our member programs. The best way to experience croquet is to visit the Abbotsford Croquet Club during regular play sessions.

Abbotsford Croquet Club

Address: Abbotsford Lawn Bowling Club
Mill Lake Park, Abbotsford, BC V2S 4S5

Play Times:
Tuesday evenings
Thursday evenings

Getting There: The club is located at Mill Lake Park in Abbotsford. Parking is available at the park. Look for the Abbotsford Lawn Bowling Club facility.

New players are welcome. Equipment and instruction provided. For specific timing and to confirm session availability, please contact the club in advance.
